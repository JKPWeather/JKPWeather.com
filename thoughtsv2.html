8/2/2020

Today in the boston area will be hot, with temperatures in the low-90s. The clouds from Tropical Storm Isaias will likely hold off until after sunset, so it should be a clear and sunny day. Dew points in the mid-70s this morning will give way to dew points in the low- to mid-60s later, so humidity will not be a huge factor. Heat indices will likely be 1-3 degrees above the actual temperature. Tomorrow, it will be cloudy by morning, but rain and wind holds off until night. Temperatures will be in the low-80s.
