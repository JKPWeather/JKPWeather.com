7/21/2020

Today will be warm and dry. A ridge is centered just south of us, bringing warm, westerly winds. Highs will approach 90, except on the Cape and Islands where highs will stay in the low 80s. Heat advisories will not be needed as they were yesterday and Sunday, as dew points today stay in the lower 60s. Tomorrow, highs will top out in the lower 80s inland, while they will stay in the upper 70s at the coast.
